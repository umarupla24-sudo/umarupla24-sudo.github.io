MP Doors 3/4 Lite Hinged Patio Door

Eight configurations. One composite standard.

MP Doors 3/4 Lite Hinged Patio Door

For buyers who want grille detailing, specific swing direction, or the exact handing their rough opening requires — eight configurations in one product line, no custom ordering needed.

Most hinged patio doors give you a handful of configurations and send you to a custom order program for the rest. The 3/4 Lite Hinged door is built for buyers who need the flexibility to match a specific rough opening, a specific swing direction, or a home with Grilles Between Glass or Simulated Divided Lite detailing. Eight distinct configurations. Inswing or outswing. Left-hand or right-hand. Standard clear glass or GBG or SDL detailing — all in one product line, all available as stock configurations. The composite frame underneath all of it is engineered with HydroShield Technology, and the Energy Star qualification applies in every U.S. climate zone.

Why This Door Performs

Eight Configurations in Stock

Eight Configurations in Stock

Available in 60-inch and 72-inch widths, inswing and outswing, left-hand and right-hand configurations — eight combinations without custom ordering.

Which means:

Whether your rough opening calls for a left-hand outswing or a right-hand inswing, the configuration you need is available without lead time or a special order.

GBG and SDL Glass Options

GBG and SDL Glass Options

Available with standard clear glass, Grilles Between Glass (GBG), and Simulated Divided Lites (SDL) — three glass detailing options within one product line.

Which means:

Buyers in homes with architectural glass detailing — craftsman profiles, colonial grilles, or traditional divided-lite patterns — have a matching option without going custom.

Both Panels Operable — No Center Post

Both Panels Operable — No Center Post

Both the main active panel and the secondary passive panel open fully, with no center post dividing the opening.

Which means:

The full clear opening is available when you need it.

Composite Frame — HydroShield Technology

Composite Frame — HydroShield Technology

The frame, panels, and all six sides are engineered with HydroShield Technology — 100% waterproof construction that resists rotting, warping, splitting, delaminating, denting, and rusting.

Which means:

The frame holds its original geometry through temperature cycling and seasonal moisture exposure, season after season.

Energy Star — All Four Climate Zones

Energy Star — All Four Climate Zones

Certified in Northern, North Central, South Central, and Southern zones — without modification or a regional product variant.

Which means:

This door meets federal thermal efficiency standards wherever in the U.S. you are installing it.

Built for Where You Live

Eight configurations means this door covers the full range of rough opening requirements, swing preferences, and regional installation codes without custom ordering. The Energy Star qualification in all four climate zones means the thermal performance is certified whether you are in a cold northern climate or a hot coastal environment.

The composite construction handles both extremes — dimensional stability through freeze-thaw cycles in northern winters, and moisture and UV resistance in southern and coastal climates — without a maintenance cycle to keep it performing.

Warranty

The frame, panels, and astragals carry a Limited Lifetime Warranty for the original purchaser — active for as long as you own your home. Glass seal coverage runs 20 years prorated; the locking mechanism is covered for 10 years. The factory painted finish carries a 2-year warranty.

Labor is not covered, and warranty requires installation by a licensed and bonded contractor. If damage is visible on delivery, contact MP Doors within 5 calendar days — call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com.
