Built to Last. Engineered to Perform.

HydroShield Technology

HydroShield Technology

HydroShield Technology seals all surfaces against moisture. The composite frame, panels, and every edge are engineered 100% waterproof — so moisture has no entry point. No corner to rot, no edge to split.

Which means:

The door you install today looks and operates the same in year fifteen, without a maintenance appointment to keep it there.

Stable Composite Frames

Stable Composite Frames

The composite frame holds its original geometry through heat, cold, and humidity. Hardware stays aligned, seals maintain compression, and the door operates the same in winter as it did on installation day.

Which means:

You are not calling a contractor in year seven because the door no longer closes properly.

Energy Star — All Zones

Energy Star — All Zones

Qualified across all four U.S. climate zones: Northern, North Central, South Central, and Southern.

Which means:

The same door meets federal thermal performance standards whether you are building in Minnesota or Texas.
