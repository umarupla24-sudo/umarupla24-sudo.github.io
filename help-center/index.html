Help Center

Answers before you buy. Solutions if something goes wrong.

Before You Buy

MP Doors patio doors come in standard sizes of 60 in. × 80 in. and 72 in. × 80 in. for double-door configurations, and 30 in. × 80 in. and 36 in. × 80 in. for single hinged doors. Note that nominal dimensions differ slightly from actual unit dimensions — a 72 in. × 80 in. door ships at 71-1/4 in. × 79-1/2 in. Measure your rough opening before ordering and confirm with your installer. Custom sizes are available through The Home Depot's Custom Door program on select gliding models.

The composite frame does not absorb moisture or change dimension with temperature, so the door that operates correctly in October operates correctly in January. The doors carry all-zone Energy Star certification: Northern, North Central, South Central, and Southern.

The PG50 designation indicates coastal performance rating. MP Doors PG50 models are rated for coastal applications — higher wind-load resistance suitable for homes near coastlines or in areas with elevated exposure. The MasterPiece series covers standard residential applications. If you are in a coastal windstorm zone requiring impact certification, see the Impact Hinged Patio Door — it carries FBC# FL-29714 and TDI certification.

They are available on select gliding patio door models. If built-in blinds are a priority, confirm the specific SKU includes them before ordering — not all configurations carry the option.

Delivery and Installation

Inspect the door before your delivery crew leaves and document any damage immediately. Report damage to MP Doors within 5 calendar days of receipt — call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com. Do not attempt to return the door to a retail store. Claims reported after 5 days may not be eligible for coverage. Early reporting is the only step within your control.

Standard gliding patio door configurations include the handle set, lock, adjustable stainless steel rollers and track, weather stripping, and installation instructions. Screen doors are sold separately and are not included. Some buyers have reported missing installation hardware such as screws, leveling blocks, and screw hole covers — inspect the box contents against the installation instructions before your contractor begins work. Contact 1-888-366-7717 if components are missing.

Confirm your rough opening dimensions match the door's nominal size before the door ships — do not assume they match. A 72 in. × 80 in. door has an actual unit dimension of 71-1/4 in. × 79-1/2 in. Your contractor should verify the opening is square, plumb, and level. Have shims, fasteners, and flashing materials on site before installation day.

No. Screen doors are sold separately for all MP Doors patio door configurations. This is a consistent point of surprise for buyers — plan for it before purchase. Confirm screen compatibility with your specific model and SKU before ordering a separate screen.

Warranty and Support

The frame, door panels, and astragals are covered by a Limited Lifetime Warranty for the original purchaser, for as long as they own the home. The locking mechanism is covered for 10 years. The glass seal is covered for 20 years prorated on non-impact doors (10 years prorated on impact doors). The factory painted finish is covered for 2 years. Built-in blind glass seals: 5 years prorated; magnetic components: 1 year.

Labor costs are not covered — you are responsible for installation and any repair costs. DIY installation (without a licensed and bonded contractor) affects warranty eligibility. Damage not reported within 5 calendar days of delivery is not eligible for coverage. The warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser. Pet door components carry a 1-year warranty only.

Call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com. Have your proof of purchase, installation documentation, and the contractor's license information available — these are required to establish warranty eligibility. For delivery damage specifically, the 5-day reporting window applies from the date of receipt, not the date of installation.

The locking mechanism is covered for 10 years from the date of purchase. Contact MP Doors at 1-888-366-7717 or customerservice@mpdoors.com to open a claim. Have your purchase date and proof of licensed contractor installation available. Do not attempt to modify or replace the mechanism yourself before contacting support — unauthorized modifications may affect coverage.

After Installation

The adjustable stainless steel rollers on MP Doors gliding doors are designed to be recalibrated after installation. An adjustment screw on the roller mechanism allows you to raise or lower the door panel relative to the track. Consult the installation instructions included with your door for the specific procedure. If the door has settled and the adjustment screw no longer corrects the issue, contact 1-888-366-7717.

Composite doors require minimal maintenance. The frame and panels do not need painting, sealing, or refinishing to hold their form or appearance. Clean the surface with mild soap and water. Lubricate the hardware and roller track annually. No stripping, sanding, or surface treatment is required on a maintenance cycle the way wood doors are.

Yes — the composite surface is paintable. The factory painted finish carries a 2-year warranty. If you repaint after that period, use exterior-grade paint. Note: applying a dark paint color with a Light Reflectance Value (LRV) below 57 may affect warranty coverage on the finish — confirm with MP Doors at 1-888-366-7717 before selecting a dark exterior color.
