MP Doors Shaker Hinged 2/3 Lite Patio Door

Open both panels. No post in the middle. No compromises.

MP Doors Shaker Hinged 2/3 Lite Patio Door

For the buyer who wants the clean geometry of Shaker design without giving up the full-width opening — both panels operate independently.

Most hinged patio doors give you one working panel and one panel that stays fixed — a practical compromise that makes moving furniture, entertaining, or simply taking the garbage out a narrower exercise than it needs to be. The Shaker Hinged 2/3 Lite door is built for buyers who want both panels to open when they need the full width and close completely when they do not. The Shaker profile — flat panels, clean edges, no ornament for its own sake — gives the door the kind of presence that reads as intentional design rather than contractor-grade default. The 2/3 glass lite brings in light without turning the lower panel into a privacy problem.

Why This Door Performs

Both Panels Open — No Center Post

Both Panels Open — No Center Post

The main active panel and the secondary passive panel both operate independently.

Which means:

When you need the full clear width — moving appliances, carrying in patio furniture, or simply throwing the space open — nothing stops you at the midpoint.

Composite Frame, 100% Waterproof

Composite Frame, 100% Waterproof

Engineered with HydroShield Technology, the fiberglass door system comes with complete composite edging and full composite frames that resist rotting, warping, splitting, delaminating, denting, and rusting.

Which means:

The frame is not going to do what wood does in year seven — swell, paint-peel, or absorb the moisture that gets behind vinyl and begins the slow process of delamination.

Patented Water-Resistant Draining System

Patented Water-Resistant Draining System

The door includes a patented water-resistant draining system combined with factory-installed weather-stripping insulation.

Which means:

Water that hits the threshold in a hard rain drains away — which matters most in the climates where weather-stripping alone is not enough.

Passive Panel Secured with Flush Bolts

Passive Panel Secured with Flush Bolts

The secondary passive panel locks into both the top and bottom frame with 2 flush bolt locks.

Which means:

When only one panel is in use, the other stays fixed without relying on hardware visible from outside.

Double-Bored for Hardware-Ready Installation

Double-Bored for Hardware-Ready Installation

The door arrives double-bored — handle and deadbolt bore locations are pre-cut so a contractor can install standard or upgraded hardware without modification.

Which means:

Your installer does not need to drill the door on site, which speeds installation and reduces the risk of frame damage.

Energy Star — All Climate Zones

Energy Star — All Climate Zones

Energy Star qualified across all four U.S. climate zones.

Which means:

The same door qualifies for every climate from the Canadian border to the Gulf — without modification or upsell.

Built for Where You Live

Energy Star certification across every U.S. climate zone means this door meets federal thermal efficiency standards whether you are building in Wisconsin or building in Louisiana. The composite construction handles temperature cycling and moisture exposure without the dimensional movement that vinyl shows at seasonal extremes — and without the rot that wood accumulates in humid Southern climates over time.

The patented draining system takes on particular value in rain-heavy coastal and Pacific Northwest climates where standing water at the threshold is a season-long condition.

Warranty

The frame, panels, and astragals carry a Limited Lifetime Warranty for the original purchaser — active for as long as you own your home. Glass seal coverage runs 20 years prorated; the locking mechanism is covered for 10 years. The factory painted finish carries a 2-year warranty.

Labor is not covered, and warranty requires installation by a licensed and bonded contractor. If damage is visible on delivery, contact MP Doors within 5 calendar days — call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com.
