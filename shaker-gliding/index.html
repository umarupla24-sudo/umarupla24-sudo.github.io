MP Doors Shaker Gliding 2/3 Lite Patio Door

The sliding door that solves the problems sliding doors are known for.

MP Doors Shaker Gliding 2/3 Lite Patio Door

For buyers who want Shaker design, built-in blinds between the glass for privacy without cords, and — on select 72-inch configurations — a factory-integrated pet door that requires no field modification.

The complaints about sliding patio doors fall into a short list: the blinds cord gets tangled and eventually breaks; the pet door was cut in the field and never quite sealed properly; the lower panel invites streetside visibility in ways a hinged door does not; and full-glass designs make the room feel surveilled when you want it to feel open. The Shaker Gliding 2/3 Lite door addresses that list at the factory. Built-in blinds are installed between the glass panels — sealed from room air, operated without a cord, replaced never. The pet door is factory-integrated on select 72-inch configurations, not cut in the field. The Shaker 2/3 lite profile gives you upper-panel light with a solid lower section that addresses the privacy condition. And the composite frame underneath all of it does not run on a maintenance cycle.

Why This Door

Factory Solutions to the Problems Field Installs Create

Six reasons buyers who have lived with the limitations of standard sliding doors choose the Shaker Gliding 2/3 Lite.

Built-In Blinds Between the Glass

Blinds factory-installed between the glass panels on select configurations — sealed from room air, operated from inside the home, and protected from physical wear and dust accumulation.

Which means: on the days you want privacy at the sliding door, you close the blinds in one motion, without a cord to manage, without a separate blind panel to replace when it wears out.

Factory-Integrated Pet Door

Available on select 72×80 configurations as a factory option — not a field modification. The pet door is integrated at the factory with proper sealing.

Which means: the pet access opening has factory-precision sealing rather than field-cut tolerances — the thermal and weather performance of the door is not compromised by the cutout.

Shaker 2/3 Lite Profile — Privacy + Light

The 2/3 lite configuration gives the upper two-thirds of the door panel to glass and reserves the lower third as a solid Shaker panel.

Which means: the room gets natural light from the upper panel without the full-glass exposure that makes interiors feel visible from the outside — the solid lower section reads as Shaker detailing, not as a privacy compromise.

Adjustable Stainless Steel Rollers

The stainless steel roller system is field-adjustable after installation and at any point in the door's lifetime.

Which means: the door that slides smoothly on installation day can be recalibrated rather than replaced if it settles or if seasonal temperature cycling shifts the panel's alignment.

100% Waterproof Composite Construction

The composite frame resists rotting, warping, splitting, delaminating, denting, and rusting — applied to all six sides of the door system.

Which means: the frame holds its original geometry through every wet season and temperature extreme without a maintenance cycle to keep it there.

Energy Star — All Zones

Energy Star certified in all four U.S. climate zones.

Which means: the door meets federal thermal performance standards in every regional climate, from Minnesota winters to Gulf Coast summers.

Built for Where You Live

Factory Precision Where It Matters Most

The built-in blinds option is engineered for the privacy condition that most sliding door buyers face — a glass panel facing a deck, a neighbor's yard, or a streetside view. The blinds are sealed between the glass panes, which means the climate outside has no effect on their operation and the climate inside does not degrade them.

The factory-integrated pet door is sealed to factory tolerances rather than field-cut tolerances, which matters in climates with extreme cold or coastal humidity where a field-cut opening accumulates infiltration issues over time. Energy Star qualified in all four U.S. climate zones.

Warranty & Support

What Is Covered

Frame, panels, and astragals carry a Limited Lifetime Warranty for the original purchaser. For configurations with built-in blinds, the glass seal warranty is 5 years prorated (vs. 20 years prorated on standard glass configurations) and the blind magnetic components carry a 1-year warranty. Pet door components carry a 1-year warranty. The locking mechanism is covered for 10 years. Factory finish: 2 years. Labor is not covered. Installation by a licensed and bonded contractor is required for warranty coverage to apply. Report delivery damage within 5 calendar days — call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com.
