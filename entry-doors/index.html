What Composite Changes About an Entry Door

Finish That Holds

Composite accepts and holds paint and stain through years of weather exposure and daily use.

Which means the colour you chose on install day is still the colour in year eight, without a refinishing appointment to keep it there.

No Seasonal Sticking

The composite frame does not expand in summer heat or swell in winter humidity.

Which means the door that opens easily on day one opens exactly as easily in year ten.

Energy Star — Every Zone

Qualified across all four U.S. climate zones: Northern, North Central, South Central, Southern.

Which means your front entry meets federal thermal standards in any U.S. climate, certified.
