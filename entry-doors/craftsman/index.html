Craftsman Entry Door
MasterPiece Series

MP Doors Craftsman Entry Door

The first thing visitors see. The last thing you want to think about.

For homeowners who want authentic Craftsman character at the front door without the maintenance liability that comes with a real wood entry — four finish options, four glass choices, and a composite frame that does not demand attention every spring.

The front entry door is the one surface on the exterior of a home that visitors look directly at — not past, not around, but at. The Craftsman series gives that surface a specific, earned character: straight-edge panels, thoughtful glazing bead detail, and the kind of geometric intentionality that reads as architectural rather than builder-grade from across the street. The buyer this door is built for wants Craftsman design without the commitment that comes with real wood — without the painting cycle, without the seasonal swelling, without the refinishing conversation every few years. Why This Door

Authentic Craftsman Detailing

Designed with straight-edge panel construction and glazing bead detail — the geometric language of true Craftsman architecture.

Which means the design holds up under close inspection — the proportions and line work read as intentional to anyone who knows what they are looking at.

Four Finish Options

Available in Smooth White (paintable), Medium Oak stained, Dark Walnut stained, and Black painted.

Which means the front door finish your home's exterior calls for is a stock option, not a custom order.

Composite Frame — Zero Maintenance

The fiberglass door system is built with complete composite edging and a full composite frame — 100% waterproof, engineered not to rot, warp, split, delaminate, dent, or rust.

Which means the frame does not require painting, sealing, or inspection that a wood entry frame demands every spring.

Four Glass Configurations

Available with LowE clear, frosted, water wave, and vintage glass options.

Which means the glass that fills the Craftsman lites does not have to default to clear — buyers can choose a glass that suits both the light and the privacy condition of their specific entry.

Factory Pre-Hung Door System

Arrives as a factory-assembled, pre-hung door system — frame, door slab, and all components pre-fitted at the factory for precise tolerances.

Which means your installer is not building a frame around a slab in the field — the factory assembly eliminates the variables that produce air gaps and operating issues from day one.

Energy Star — All Zones

Energy Star certified across all four U.S. climate zones: Northern, North Central, South Central, and Southern.

Which means the front door meets federal thermal efficiency standards in every region.

Built for Where You Live

An entry door takes weather from a different angle than a patio door — it faces the street, catches direct wind, and in many climates takes the full force of rain, ice, and UV without the shelter that a covered patio provides. The composite frame handles all of those exposures without the deterioration timeline that wood entry frames run on in northern climates, or that painted steel frames accumulate in coastal environments.

Energy Star certification in all four U.S. climate zones means this is not a door built for one regional market — it is built to meet thermal performance requirements from Minnesota to Texas without modification.

Warranty & Support

The frame, panels, and structural components carry a Limited Lifetime Warranty for the original purchaser — valid for as long as you own your home. The glass seal is covered for 20 years prorated. The locking mechanism carries a 10-year warranty. The factory painted finish (including the Black painted option) is covered for 2 years. Labor is not covered, and installation must be performed by a licensed and bonded contractor for warranty coverage to apply. Damage visible on delivery must be reported within 5 calendar days — call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com.
