If Your Door Arrives Damaged

1

Inspect on delivery

Before your delivery crew leaves, inspect the door for visible damage. Document any damage with photos immediately — before the crew departs. You cannot add documentation after the fact.

2

Report within 5 calendar days

Contact MP Doors within 5 calendar days of receipt — not the date of installation. Call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com. This window is firm.

3

Do not return to store

Do not attempt to return a damaged door to a retail location. MP Doors handles the claim directly — you will not be asked to transport a heavy door back to the store. The process starts with a phone call.

How to Make a Warranty Claim

1

Gather your documentation

Collect proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation), your contractor's name and license number, and documentation of the issue. These are required to establish warranty eligibility.

2

Contact MP Doors directly

Call 1-888-366-7717 or email customerservice@mpdoors.com. Do not go through the retailer for warranty claims — contact MP Doors directly.

3

Wait for resolution

MP Doors will evaluate the claim and determine coverage. For covered claims, replacement components or units are provided. Labor costs are not covered under any warranty claim.

Professional Installation Required

Warranty coverage on all MP Doors products requires installation by a licensed and bonded contractor. This requirement protects buyers by establishing a professional installation record if claims arise. DIY installation does not void the product itself — but it does affect your ability to make a warranty claim. If you are uncertain whether your installer qualifies, confirm their license status before installation day.
